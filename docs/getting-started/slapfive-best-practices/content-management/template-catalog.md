# Template Catalog

**Full page boards**: These are used for sharing content such as recorded references, case study 2.0 served up to sales to share with prospects.  In our SFDC app sharing the storyboards will attribute back to influenced revenue when shared from an opportunity that is closed won.&#x20;

**Widgets:** Embedded into your website to look and feel as if they are a part of your website. Can be on a landing page or embedded in a blog post. **Learn more** [**here.**](../../../customizations/embed-customer-voice-on-your-website-or-landing-page.md)

All boards and widgets are branded with your colors, logos, and font choices.
