# Customer Invite Form Examples

Any form will work to integrate for your Customer Invite form

**Most used: Google forms, Hubspot, Survey Monkey, Airtable, Asana, SmartSheet**

* You need to include First Name, Last Name, Email and Company at a minimum.&#x20;
* The other criteria you want to collect is their activity types willing to do - and have them match to the backend user settings of SlapFive.

_**Learn more about Form Completion to SlapFive Member Zapier Integration**_ [_**here.**_](https://app.gitbook.com/o/E3q0uQoFPqnviXp69kaC/s/lkxMKaLwhXP9Cu98jtv6/~/changes/0KWRCzNGGElSpo7W4QSL/integrations/zapier/form-builder-zaps/form-completion-greater-than-slapfive-member)
