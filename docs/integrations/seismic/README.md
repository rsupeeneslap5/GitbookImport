# Seismic

The SlapFive integration with Seismic lets you automate the posting of the SlapFive customer content that you want to put into the hands of your sales team into a specific location in the Seismic content library. Your sales team can then search and filter in Seismic to find the right Boards and Stories, and share them with prospects.
