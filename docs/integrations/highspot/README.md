# Highspot

The SlapFive integration with Highspot lets you automate the posting of the SlapFive customer content that you want to put into the hands of your sales team into specific Highspot “Spots”. Your sales team can then search and filter in Highspot to find the right Boards and Stories, and share them with prospects.
